IntelliTrain: Intelligent Sports Training and Motion Analysis System

Chenxuan Ji1, Jingqi Liu2, Lina Sun1*

1College of P.E. and Sports, Beijing Normal University, 2Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University

*Corresponding Author

Abstract

This paper presents a novel intelligent sports training and motion analysis system (IntelliTrain), which combines advanced computer vision technology and artificial intelligence algorithms to analyze athletes' movements, postures, and performance in real-time. The system utilizes multiple visual analysis models, including 3D human mesh reconstruction, precise human pose estimation, and temporal action recognition, to provide comprehensive motion analysis and training guidance for coaches and athletes. Experiments demonstrate that the system exhibits excellent performance and practical value in various sports, effectively improving training efficiency and athletic performance.
